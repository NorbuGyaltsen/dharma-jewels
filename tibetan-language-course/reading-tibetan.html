Subscribed Letters

Spelling Recitation Order: Subscript Letter + Root Letter + TAK = Complete Sound

  • YA-TAK
  • RA-TAK
  • LA-TAK (all "la", except: ཟླ་ pronounced "da")
  • WA-ZUR (no change to sound)

Superscripted Letters

Spelling Recitation Order: Superscript Letter + Root Letter + TAK = Complete Sound

  • When ཀ་ཅ་ཏ་པ་ཙ་ receive a superscript there is no change in the original tone.

  • When ག་ཇ་ད་བ་ཛ་ receive a superscript they lose aspiration.

  • When ང་ཉ་ན་མ་ receive a superscript they have a higher tone.

  • RAN-GO, LAN-GO, SAN-GO (no change from root letter sounds other than main rules, with the exception of one of the LAN-GO's)
