The Aspiration Prayer of Mahamudra, the Definitive Meaning

By the Third Karmapa, Rangjung Dorje

Namo Guru

LA MA NAM DANG YI DAM KYIL KHOR LHA
Gurus and yidams, deities of the mandala,

CHOK CHU DÜ SUM GYEL WA SÉ DANG CHÉ
Buddhas and bodhisattvas of the three times and ten directions,

DAK LA TSER GONG DAK GI MÖN LAM NAM
Please think of me with love and grant your blessings.

JI SHIN DRUP PÉ TÜN GYUR JIN LAB DZÖ
May this prayer be perfectly fulfilled.

DAK DANG TA YÉ SEM CHEN TAM CHÉ KYI
Springing from the snow mountain of pure thoughts and deeds,

SAM JOR NAM DAK GANG RI LÉ KYÉ PÉ
Both my own and those of all sentient beings,

KHOR SUM NYOK MÉ GÉ TSOK CHU GYÜN NAM
May the stream of accumulated virtue, untainted by the three spheres,

GYEL WA KU SHI GYA TSOR JUK GYUR CHIK
Flow into the ocean of the four kayas of the victorious ones.

JI SI DÉ MA TOP PA DÉ SI DU
Until this has come to pass,

KYÉ DANG KYÉ WA TSÉ RAP KÜN TU YANG
In all our future lives,

DIK DANG DUK NGAL DRA YANG MI DRAK SHING
May we not even hear the words “misdeeds” and “suffering.”

DÉ GÉ GYA TSÖ PAL LA JÖ PAR SHOK
May we enjoy the glorious ocean of happiness and virtue.

TEL JOR CHOK TOP DÉ TSÖN SHÉ RAP DEN
With the supreme freedoms and riches, with faith, diligence, and wisdom,

SHÉ NYEN ZANG TEN DAM PÉ CHÜ TOP NÉ
May we rely on excellent teachers and receive their pith instructions.

TSÜL SHIN DRUP LA PAR CHÉ MA CHI PAR
Free from any obstacles in practicing them correctly,

TSÉ RAP KÜN TU DAM CHÖ JÖ PAR SHOK
May we practice the sacred Dharma in all our lives.

LUNG RIK TÖ PÉ MI SHÉ DRIP LÉ DRÖL
Studying scripture and reasoning lifts the veil of ignorance.

MEN NGAK SAM PÉ TÉ TSOM MÜN NAK CHOM
Pondering the key instructions dispels the darkness of doubt.

GOM JUNG Ö KYI NÉ LUK JI SHIN SEL
The light of meditation illuminates the true nature of things.

SHÉ RAP SUM GYI NANG WA GYÉ PAR SHOK
May the radiance of the three forms of wisdom expand.

TAK CHÉ TA DREL DEN NYI SHI YI DÖN
Through the nature of the ground, the two truths, beyond the extremes of permanence and nothingness,

DRO KUR TA DREL TSOK NYI LAM CHOK GI
And through the supreme path of the two accumulations, free from the limitations of exaggeration and denial,

SI SHI TA DREL DÖN NYI DRÉ TOP PÉ
We attain the result of the two-fold benefit, beyond the confines of existence and peace.

KÖL CHUK MÉ PÉ CHÖ DANG TRÉ PAR SHOK
May we meet with the unerring, unmistaken Dharma.

JANG SHI SEM NYI SEL TONG SUNG JUK LA
The basis of purification is the mind itself, the union of clarity and emptiness.

JONG JÉ CHAK CHEN DOR JÉ NAL JOR CHÉ
What purifies is the supreme vajra yoga of Mahāmudrā

JANG CHA LO PUR TRÜL PÉ DRI MA NAM
And what it purifies are the temporary stains of confusion.

JANG DRÉ DRI DREL CHÖ KU NGÖN GYUR SHOK
May the pristine dharmakāya, the result of purification, become manifest.

SHI LA DRO DOK CHÖ PA TA WÉ DENG
Confidence in the view cuts through assumptions about the ground.

DÉ LA MA YENG KYONG WA GOM PÉ NÉ
The key point of meditation is to maintain the view without distraction.

GOM DÖN KÜN LA TSEL JONG JÖ PÉ CHOK
The supreme conduct is to master all the points of meditation.

TA GOM JÖ PÉ DENG DANG DEN PAR SHOK
May we gain confidence in the view, meditation, and conduct.

CHÖ NAM TAM CHÉ SEM KYI NAM TRÜL TÉ
All phenomena are projections of the mind.

SEM NI SEM MÉ SEM KYI NGO WÖ TONG
Yet there is no mind; it is empty of any essence.

TONG SHING MA GAK CHIR YANG NANG WA TÉ
Empty yet unobstructed, it can manifest as anything.

LEK PAR TAK NÉ SHI TSA CHÖ PAR SHOK
Investigating it with care, may we cut the very root of the mind.

YÖ MA NYONG WÉ RANG NANG YÜL DU TRÜL
Non-existent self-appearances are mistaken for objects.

MA RIG WANG GI RANG RIK DAK TU TRÜL
Due to ignorance, self-awareness is mistaken for a self.

NYI DZIN WANG GI SI PÉ LONG DU KHYAM
We wander through saṃsāra under the sway of dualistic fixation.

MA RIK TRÜL PÉ TSÉ DAR CHÖ PAR SHOK
May we completely cut the root of ignorance and confusion.

YÖ PA MA YIN GYEL WÉ KYANG MA SIK
Mind does not exist, for even the buddhas do not see it.

MÉ PA MA YIN KHOR DÉ KÜN GYI SHI
Yet neither does it not exist; it is the basis of all saṃsāra and nirvāṇa.

GEL DU MA YIN SUNG JUK U MÉ LAM
This is not a contradiction, but a unity, the middle way.

TA DREL SEM KYI CHÖ NYI TOK PAR SHOK
May we realize the true nature of our minds, free from extremes.

DI YIN SHÉ PA GANG GI TSÖN PA MÉ
It cannot be characterized by saying what it is.

DI MIN SHÉ CHA GANG GI KAK PA MÉ
It cannot be refuted by saying what it is not.

LO LÉ DÉ PÉ CHÖ NYI DÜ MA CHÉ
Unconditioned reality transcends conceptual mind.

YANG DAG DÖN GYI TA NI NGÉ PAR SHOK
May we come to understand the extent of the genuine truth.

DI NYI MA TOK KHOR WÉ GYA TSOR KHOR
When not realized, we circle through the ocean of saṃsāra.

DI NYI TOK NA SANG GYÉ SHEN NA MÉ
When it is, buddhahood is found nowhere else.

TAM CHÉ DI YIN DI MIN GANG YANG MÉ
Everything is this. There is nothing at all that is not.

CHÖ NYI KÜN SHI TSANG NI RIK PAR SHOK
May we come to know this reality, the secret of the all-ground.

NANG YANG SEM LA TONG YANG SEM YIN TÉ
Appearances are mind. Emptiness is mind.

TOK KYANG SEM LA TRÜL YANG RANG GI SEM
Realization is mind. Confusion is mind.

KYÉ KYANG SEM LA GAK KYANG SEM YIN PÉ
Arising is mind. Cessation is mind.

DRO DOK TAM CHÉ SEM LA CHÖ PAR SHOK
May we cut through all our assumptions about the mind.

LÖ CHÉ TSÖL WÉ GOM GYI MA LÉ CHING
Untainted by forced, conceptual meditation,

TA MEL DU DZI LUNG GI MA KYÖ PAR
Unmoved by the winds of mundane distractions,

MA CHÖ NYUK MA RANG BAP JOK SHÉ PÉ
Knowing how to rest naturally and uncontrived in our basic nature,

SEM DÖN NYAM LEN KHÉ SHING KYONG WAR SHOK
May we master maintaining the experience of the nature of mind.

TRA RAK TOK PÉ BA LAP RANG SAR SHI
With the waves of coarse and subtle thoughts subsiding on their own,

YÖ MÉ SEM KYI CHU WO NGANG GI NÉ
And the calm waters of the mind resting naturally,

JING MUG NYOK PÉ DRI MA DANG DREL WÉ
May we be free from the problems of dullness and lethargy

SHI NÉ GYA TSO MI YO TEN PAR SHOK
And stabilize the still ocean of calm abiding.

DAR MÉ SEM LA YANG YANG TÉ PÉ TSÉ
Looking again and again at the mind that cannot be seen,

TONG MÉ DÖN NI JI SHIN LHAK KER TONG
What cannot be seen is seen clearly just as it is.

YIN MIN DöN lA Té TsOM cHö PA NYI Cutting through doubts about what it is and what it is not,

TRÜL MÉ RANG NGO RANG GI SHÉ PAR SHOK
May we come to know our own nature without confusion.

YÜL LA TÉ PÉ YÜL MÉ SEM SU TONG
Looking at objects, there are none; they are seen to be mind.

SEM LA TÉ PÉ SEM MÉ NGO WÖ TONG
Looking at mind, there is none; it is empty of any essence.

NYI LA TÉ PÉ NYI DZIN RANG SAR DRÖL
Looking at both, dualistic fixation is freed on its own.

Ö SEL SEM KYI NÉ LUK TOK PAR SHOK
May we realize luminosity, the true nature of mind.

YI CHÉ DREL WA DI NI CHAK GYA CHÉ
Beyond mental activity, it is the Great Seal.

TA DANG DREL WA U MA CHEN PO YIN
Free from extremes, it is the Great Middle Way

DI NI KÜN DÜ DZOK CHEN SHÉ KYANG CHA
All encompassing, it is the Great Perfection.

CHIK SHÉ KÜN DÖN TOK PÉ DENG TOP SHOK
May we gain confidence in realizing all through knowing this one point.

SHEN PA MÉ PÉ DÉ CHEN GYÜN CHÉ MÉ
Unceasing great bliss, free of fixation,

TSEN DZIN MÉ PÉ Ö SEL DRIP YOK DREL
Unobscured luminosity, beyond clinging to concepts,

LO LÉ DÉ PÉ MI TOK LHÜN GYI DRUP
Spontaneously present nonthought, transcending mind –

TSÖL MÉ NYAM NYONG GYÜN CHÉ MÉ PAR SHOK
May the stream of effortless experience flow without ceasing.

ZANG SHEN NYAM KYI DZIN PA RANG SAR DRÖL
Clinging to good experiences is freed on its own.

NGEN TOK TRÜL PA RANG SHIN YING SU DAK
The confusion of negative thoughts is naturally purified into basic space.

TA MEL SHÉ PA PANG LANG DREL TOP MÉ
Ordinary mind is beyond being accepted or rejected; it cannot be lost or gained.

DRÖ DREL CHÖ NYI DEN PA TOK PAR SHOK
May we realize this freedom from concepts, the truth of reality itself.

DRO WÉ RANG SHIN TAK TU SANG GYÉ KYANG
Beings have always been buddhas by their very nature.

MA TOK WANG GI TA MÉ KHOR WAR KHYAM
Yet not knowing this they wander endlessly in saṃsāra.

DUG NGAL MU TA MÉ PÉ SEM CHEN LA
For those beings who experience suffering without end,

ZÖ MÉ NYING JÉ GYÜ LA KYÉ WAR SHOK
May overwhelming compassion take birth in our minds.

ZÖ MÉ NYING JÉ TSEL YANG MA GAK PÉ
In the moment of love, with the unceasing force of unbearable compassion,

TSÉ DÜ NGO WO TONG DÖN JEN PAR SHAR
The truth of the empty essence nakedly dawns.

SUNG JUK GÖL SA DREL WÉ LAM CHOK DI
This unity is the unerring supreme path.

DREL MÉ NYIN TSEN KÜN TU GOM PAR SHOK
May we never be separate from it, meditating day and night.

GOM TOP LÉ JUNG JEN DANG NGÖN SHÉ DANG
With the vision and direct perception born of meditation,

SEM CHEN MIN CHÉ SANG GYÉ SHING RAP JANG
Maturing sentient beings and establishing buddha fields,

SANG GYÉ CHÖ NAM DRUP PÉ MÖN LAM DZOK
And fulfilling the aspirations that bring the qualities of the buddhas,

DZOK MIN JANG SUM TAR CHIN SANG GYÉ SHOK
May we attain buddhahood, where fulfilling, maturing, and establishing are complete.

CHOK CHÜ GYEL WA SÉ CHÉ TUK JÉ DANG
Through the power of the buddhas in the ten directions and their heirs,

NAM KAR GÉ WA JI NYÉ YÖ PÉ TÜ
And whatever wholesome virtue we may possess,

DÉ TAR DAK DANG SEM CHEN TAM CHÉ KYI
May my own pure aspirations

MÖN LAM NAM DAK JI SHIN DRUP GYUR CHIK
And those of all beings be fulfilled.
